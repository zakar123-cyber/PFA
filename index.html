About Us

Flashway saw a rise in recognition during the 1960s when its name appeared on railway schedules and travel brochures, and again in the 90s as digital ticketing systems integrated the brand into their platforms. Today, Flashway is a staple of modern transit, featured on travel apps, station displays, and route planners. Book your next journey with us or explore the rich history of Flashway’s rail innovation.
